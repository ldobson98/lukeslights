Each year, Luke's Lights organizes a project for viewers to give back to our local community. In previous years, we have sponsored a food drive; this year, our campaign is Pay It Forward, a low-to-no-cost activity with many ways to participate.

Monetary donation: donate to a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization this holiday season.
Food drive donation: donate to your local food pantry or a food drive in December. Please note: Luke's Lights will not be collecting food on property in 2020.
Act of kindness: spread positivity in the community this winter by performing acts of kindness (e.g. holding the door open for someone, paying for the next person's coffee in the drive-thru, or volunteering time with a non-profit).
