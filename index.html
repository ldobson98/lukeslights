Introduction

Welcome to Luke’s Lights! We are a computerized, residential display located in New Hampshire. Our display has about 8,500 lights, the majority of which are energy-efficient LEDs. All of our lights are synchronized to music over our own radio station. In 2012, we added a food donation box, where non-perishable food was collected for the St. Joseph’s Food Pantry. To date, we've collected more than 24,000 pounds of food, including 5,762 pounds in 2019 alone! The Luke’s Lights team encourages you to like us on Facebook, where you will find progress updates.
