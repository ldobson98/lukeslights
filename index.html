Introduction

Welcome to Luke’s Lights! We are a computerized, residential display located in New Hampshire. Our display has about 8,500 lights, the majority of which are energy-efficient LEDs. All of our lights are synchronized to music over our own radio station. In 2012, we added a food donation box, where non-perishable food was collected for the St. Joseph’s Food Pantry. To date, we've collected more than 24,000 pounds of food, including 5,762 pounds in 2019 alone! This year's service project will be Pay It Forward -- see below for details. The Luke’s Lights team encourages you to like us on Facebook to see progress updates.

Pay It Forward

Each year, Luke's Lights organizes a project for viewers to give back to our local community. In previous years, we have sponsored a food drive; this year, our campaign is Pay It Forward, a low-to-no-cost activity with many ways to participate. Pay It Forward activities can be logged on our website once the show begins.

Monetary donation: donate to a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization this holiday season.
Food drive donation: donate to your local food pantry or food drive in December. Please note: Luke's Lights will not be collecting food on property in 2020.
Act of kindness: spread positivity in the community this winter by performing acts of kindness (e.g. holding the door open for someone, paying for the next person's coffee in the drive-thru, or volunteering time with a non-profit).

Post pictures of your Pay It Forward activities on Instagram, and tag @LukesLightsNH and #PayItForward for a chance to be featured on our story!
