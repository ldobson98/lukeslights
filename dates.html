Dates of Operation

We're open from November 30 - December 31, pending no storms. The display will end in advance of any major storm. We have the following hours:

  • Sunday - Thursday: 4:30p to 9:30p ET
  • Friday and Saturday: 4:30p to 10:30p ET

The busiest days of the week are Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. Long lines, especially during these days, may result in a truncated show. We encourage you to come during a weeknight for the best experience. Below is a calendar indicating our forecast for the busiest and least-busiest nights. Use it to help plan your visit!

INCLEMENT WEATHER POLICY: During periods of snow, rain, or other inclement weather, the show may be shut down. Please keep an eye on our home page and Facebook page for the latest updates. Generally, snow is not an issue for the show: rain creates many more problems for the lights.

Food Pantry Information

Since 2012, we have collected non-perishable food items for the St. Joseph's Food Pantry. We encourage you to drop off a canned good in the bin located at the bottom of the driveway during show hours. Thanks for helping us reach our goal.

Rules & Regulations

We have the following rules, and we'd greatly appreciate your assistance in following them. Thank you!

  • Do not beep your horn.
  • If you plan on staying for some time, please turn off your engine. Bring blankets; it gets cold!
  • Don't litter! We ask you not to litter because it may cause an electrical problem.
  • Be considerate of our neighbors. Please do not turn around in our neighbors driveways. Our neighborhood consists of two cul-de-sacs, as well as two entrance and exit points. Keep going, turning left or right, and you'll eventually find your way out.
  • Turn off your lights. I know some cars can't, however, if you know your can, please do so if you plan on pulling over to watch the show.
  • If you happen to have any non-expired and non-perishable food items that you would be willing to donate, please bring them with you. We will have a collection box for the St. Joseph's Food Pantry.
  • And lastly, ENJOY THE SHOW! When seeing the show, feel free to tweet us or post on our Facebook page! After you've seen the show, please take the poll on our home page to tell us which song you liked the most!
