We continuted to have many people stop by in 2013. We set a goal of 1,000 pounds for our Food Drive, and reached a grand total of 1,380 pounds! New this year were our leaping arches, which are super smart: we can turn on each light individually to any color we wish! We added "Let It Go" from Disney's latest hit, Frozen, which was a quick fan favorite. We had about 8,000 lights in our fourth year.
