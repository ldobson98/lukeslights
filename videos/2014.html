This year's additions were our flood lights and our icicle tubes. Combined, these features added a huge amount of flexibility to the show, and kept the wow-factor high. We collected 1,735 pounds of food through our annual food drive, which topped our goal of 1,500 pounds. City Hall also debuted its Holiday Spectacular this year. In all, Luke's Lights had more than 8,000 lights in 2014.
